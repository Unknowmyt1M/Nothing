Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2025
Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

Google Account Information
  • Basic Profile Data: Name, email address, and Google profile ID when you authenticate with Google OAuth
  • YouTube API Access: Permission to upload videos to your YouTube channel
  • Authentication Tokens: Temporary access tokens to interact with YouTube API on your behalf
Video and Platform Data
  • Video URLs: Links to videos you want to download from supported platforms
  • Video Metadata: Title, description, thumbnails, and other public information from source videos
  • Upload Settings: Privacy settings, titles, and descriptions you provide for YouTube uploads
Technical Information
  • Session Data: Temporary data stored during your browsing session
  • Progress Information: Download and upload progress data (automatically deleted after completion)
  • Error Logs: Technical error information to improve service reliability

How We Use Your Information

Core Functionality
  • Download videos from supported platforms
  • Extract and process video metadata
  • Upload videos to your YouTube channel
  • Track upload progress and status
Service Improvement
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Improve platform compatibility
  • Enhance user experience
  • Monitor service performance

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
Limited Sharing Scenarios
  • Google/YouTube: We share only necessary data with YouTube API to upload videos on your behalf
  • Platform APIs: We access public video metadata from supported platforms (YouTube, Instagram, etc.)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

Data Storage and Security

Storage Practices
  • Temporary Files: Downloaded videos are automatically deleted after upload
  • Session Data: Stored temporarily in encrypted sessions
  • User Preferences: Minimal data stored in user directories
Security Measures
  • OAuth 2.0: Secure Google authentication
  • HTTPS: All data transmission encrypted
  • Token Management: Automatic token refresh and expiration

Cookies and Tracking

Platform Cookies
  • Authentication Cookies: Used to access restricted content on some platforms
  • Session Cookies: Essential for maintaining your login state
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies for advertising or user tracking
Third-Party Services
  • Google OAuth: Handles authentication securely
  • Platform APIs: Direct communication with video platforms
  • No Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking services

Your Rights and Controls

Account Control
  • Disconnect: Revoke Google account access anytime
  • Data Deletion: Request removal of your data
  • Access Control: Control what data you share
Privacy Settings
  • Upload Privacy: Choose public, unlisted, or private for uploads
  • Metadata Control: Edit titles and descriptions before upload
  • Consent Management: Clear consent for all data processing

Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period Purpose
Video Files Deleted immediately after upload Temporary processing only
Session Data Until logout or expiration Maintain user session
Access Tokens Until revoked or expired YouTube API access
Error Logs 30 days maximum Troubleshooting and improvement

International Users

This service is hosted on Replit's infrastructure. By using our service, you acknowledge that your data may be processed in countries where Replit operates, including the United States.

Children's Privacy

Age Restriction: This service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify users of significant changes through the application
  • Continue to protect your data according to the updated policy

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:

  • For general inquiries: Use the feedback option in your Replit account
  • For data requests: Contact through the accounts page
  • For technical issues: Report through the application interface
