Copyright & DMCA Policy

Protecting intellectual property rights

Overview

UpDownVid respects intellectual property rights and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

We do NOT store or host videos. We only facilitate downloads from third-party platforms.

DMCA Takedown Process

If you believe your copyrighted work has been infringed, please send a DMCA notice to:

Email: copyright@updownvid.com
Your notice must include:
  1. Identification of copyrighted work - Clear description of the work you claim has been infringed
  2. Infringing material location - URL or specific location of the allegedly infringing content
  3. Contact information - Name, address, phone number, and email
  4. Good faith statement - Statement that you believe the use is not authorized
  5. Accuracy statement - Statement under penalty of perjury that the information is accurate
  6. Signature - Physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized agent

Counter-Notification

If you believe content was removed by mistake, you may file a counter-notice including:

  • Your contact information
  • Identification of removed content and its location
  • Statement under penalty of perjury that removal was a mistake
  • Consent to jurisdiction of federal court
  • Your signature

Repeat Infringer Policy

UpDownVid will terminate accounts of users who repeatedly infringe copyright:

1st Violation
Warning

2nd Violation
Suspension

3rd Violation
Permanent Ban

User Responsibilities

As a user, you must:

  • Only download content you have rights to
  • Respect copyright and fair use laws
  • Obtain necessary permissions
  • Don't download copyrighted material
  • Don't re-upload others' content
  • Don't claim ownership of others' work
